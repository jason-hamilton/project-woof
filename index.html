Identifying a Poodle

The poodle is categorized into three types: standard, miniature,and toy. Normally, poodles have evenly proportioned, clipped, and designed fur. Stemming from the working retriever breed, poodles have an athletic build.

Types of Poodles

  • Standard
  • Miniature
  • Toy

Poodle Behavior

The Standard Poodle is known as one of the most obdedient and smart dogs. It is highly athletic. It enjoys running, walking, swimming, and hunting. It is a very shy dog, but it behaves well around children. As for the Miniature and Toy Poodle, they are extremly playful and energetic. They are very obedient and are very popular.

Nutritional Facts

A nutritionally sound diet for your poodle includes a variety of ingredients. That can include historically significant staples such as fish and poultry, along with other meats. Alongside those protein items, your poodle should get some vegetables, healthy fats and oils, some carbs such as starchy vegetables, fruits, and even some grains such as rice or oatmeal. Creating the right ratios of each food will help your poodle live to his fullest.
